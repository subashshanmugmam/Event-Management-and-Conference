Why Attend

Build tomorrow's events today

From immersive mainstage moments to curated networking, Eventia Summit fuels your creativity with actionable strategies.

Visionary Insights

60+ mainstage talks from industry pioneers including Fortune 500 executives, award-winning designers, and tech innovators shaping the future of hybrid and experiential events. Learn strategies that drove 300% ROI increases.

Hands-on Labs

Experiment with AI-powered analytics, immersive AR/VR experiences, and real-time engagement platforms in fully-equipped sandbox environments. Expert-led sessions with live production setups and streaming infrastructure.

Curated Networking

AI-matched roundtables connecting you with 20+ ideal contacts, exclusive executive lounges with C-suite leaders, speed networking sessions, and industry-specific meetups. Private meeting spaces and networking concierge included.

Global Community

Connect with 5,200+ decision-makers from 48 countries across corporate events, associations, agencies, hospitality, and technology sectors. Access our year-round community platform with exclusive resources and job opportunities.

Immersive Experiences

Explore 25+ sensory pop-ups including 360° projection mapping, haptic feedback installations, scent marketing demonstrations, and cutting-edge mixed reality showcases. Walk through fully-realized event concepts hands-on.

Certification Tracks

Earn up to 24 CMP credits (Certified Meeting Professional) across specialized tracks: strategic leadership, sustainable event design, production management, digital engagement, and hybrid event execution. Globally recognized certifications and digital badges.
