Eventia Summit is an approved provider for Certified Meeting Professional (CMP) credits through Events Industry Council. You can earn up to 24 credits across 8 specialized tracks.

To receive credits: Check in at each session using the event app, complete post-session surveys, and maintain 80% attendance in your chosen track. Certificates are issued within 2 weeks after the event and automatically reported to EIC for CMP maintenance.

Track your progress in real-time via the mobile app.
